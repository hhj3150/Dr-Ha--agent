# Global Holstein Embryo Export Playbook

Master workflow for the Korean **Holstein sexed dairy embryo** export business
(Central Asia + Southeast Asia, Dubai hub). Owner: Global Embryo Export Operations
Agent. Export target = Holstein dairy embryos only; **never Hanwoo**.

## 8 stages
1. **Country selection** — dairy size, Holstein demand, import rules, vet-authority
   requirements, partner availability, government cooperation, climate/management risk,
   payment/contract risk. (Use country-entry-checklist + country-entry-report-template.)
2. **Regulatory & quarantine** — import/export permits, health certificate, embryo ID,
   donor-cow records, disease testing, quarantine timeline, customs. (Use embryo-export-document-preparation.)
3. **Partner development** — partner profile, MOU, distribution & technical-service agreements,
   local-vet training, pilot proposal. (Use international-partner-mou-template.)
4. **Embryo production planning** — with Genetics Lab Operations: quantity, schedule, quality,
   freezing, inventory allocation, export-batch IDs. (Use embryo-batch-inventory-template.)
5. **Export execution** — invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, health certificate,
   embryo list, cryotank logistics, flight, customs. (Use embryo-export-logistics-management.)
6. **Local transfer project** — recipient-cow criteria, vet training, transfer & pregnancy-check
   & calving follow-up, data sheet. (Use recipient-cow-selection / pregnancy & calving trackers.)
7. **Performance analysis** — exported/transferred, conception/pregnancy/calving, female-calf,
   survival, satisfaction, repeat potential.
8. **Scale-up** — follow-up contract, country expansion, government cooperation, Eco-BIT/CowTalk
   integration, investor presentation.

## Reference market
Uzbekistan (RDA cooperation, ~3-year confirmed results — figures TBD). Use as proof model.

## Output
A staged country plan with documents, schedule, partners, KPIs, and next actions.
Mark figures as estimates/pilot; documents as drafts needing legal/veterinary review.
