# Embryo Export Logistics Management Workflow

Use to manage cold-chain logistics for a frozen-embryo export shipment. Owner: Global
Embryo Export Operations Agent.

## Steps
1. **Batch & inventory** — confirm export-batch IDs and quantities (with Genetics Lab Operations).
2. **Cryotank prep** — LN2 cryotank readiness, charge level, sealing, ID tags.
3. **Booking** — flight schedule, courier/forwarder, dangerous-goods/temperature handling.
4. **Documents** — ensure all export documents accompany the shipment (see document-preparation).
5. **Customs** — export-side and import-side clearance steps & contacts.
6. **In-transit** — tracking, contingency (delay / re-charge), recipient handoff.
7. **Delivery confirmation** — arrival, cryotank integrity, handover to local partner/vet.

## Output
A shipment plan & tracking sheet: batch list, cryotank status, flight & customs schedule,
document pack, contingency plan, delivery confirmation.

## Risks
LN2 hold-time vs transit time; customs delay; temperature excursion. Build buffer & contingency.

## Rule
Coordinate batch readiness with Genetics Lab Operations. Mark schedules as plan until booked/confirmed.
