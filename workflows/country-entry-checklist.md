# Country Entry Checklist Workflow

Use to assess and prepare entry into a target country (SE Asia: Malaysia, Indonesia,
Thailand, Philippines, Vietnam; Central Asia: Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan,
Tajikistan, Turkmenistan). Owner: Global Embryo Export Operations Agent.

## 1. Market assessment
- [ ] Dairy-industry size
- [ ] Holstein demand
- [ ] Local farm-management capability & climate risk

## 2. Regulatory
- [ ] Import regulations for bovine embryos
- [ ] Veterinary-authority requirements
- [ ] Import-permit process & timeline
- [ ] Health-certificate / disease-testing requirements

## 3. Partner & channel
- [ ] Local partner availability & profile
- [ ] Distribution model
- [ ] Local-vet capacity for ET

## 4. Government & cooperation
- [ ] Government-cooperation potential (RDA / ODA / KOICA)
- [ ] Existing programs to leverage

## 5. Commercial risk
- [ ] Payment & contract risk
- [ ] Pricing & competition
- [ ] Logistics feasibility (cryotank, flights, customs)

## Output
A completed country-entry report (use country-entry-report-template) with a go / hold /
pilot recommendation and next steps. Uzbekistan = reference benchmark.

## Rule
Mark unverified regulatory items as "to confirm with importing-country veterinary authority."
