# B2B Sales Playbook Workflow (A2 Jersey Hay Milk)

Use to win and grow B2B accounts — cafe, bakery, hotel, department store, premium
food-hall. Owner: Sales & Distribution Agent (with Product Development for price,
Brand Marketing for materials).

## Target segments
Cafe · bakery · hotel · dessert/patisserie · department-store food-hall · premium grocery.

## Pipeline
1. **Target list** — name accounts per segment & region.
2. **Qualify** — volume potential, fit (premium A2 positioning), decision-maker.
3. **Approach** — tailored pitch + B2B proposal + price quote + samples.
4. **Trial** — sampling/tasting, trial order, menu fit (e.g., A2 latte, kaymak plate).
5. **Close** — terms, MOQ, delivery cadence (cold-chain), pricing/margin.
6. **Account growth** — reorder cadence, new SKUs, co-marketing.

## Pitch angles
- A2 protein "may be easier to digest for some consumers" (no medical claims).
- Hay-milk natural forage feeding; premium provenance; Soil-to-Soul story.
- Consistent quality & cold-chain reliability.

## Required output
- Segmented target account list
- Per-account approach plan + assets (proposal, quote, samples)
- Pricing tiers & MOQ logic (mark as draft until costing confirmed)
- Pipeline tracker (stage, value, next step)
- Monthly B2B targets & KPIs

## Rule
Coordinate price/margin with Product Development; use b2b-sales-proposal-template and
peat-style ROI logic where relevant. Mark numbers as targets.
