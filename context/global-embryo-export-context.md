# Global Holstein Embryo Export — Business Context

Shared context for the Global Embryo Export Operations Agent and Genetics Lab
Operations Agent. Read with `founder-context.md`, `master-context.md`,
`confidentiality-rules.md`.

## What this business is
Korean **Holstein sexed (female) dairy embryo** export, expanded into a global
business centered on **Central Asia and Southeast Asia**, with **Dubai as the global
hub**. This is an integrated business, not simple embryo sales:
production → quarantine/export documents → overseas partners → Dubai hub →
country-by-country entry → genetics lab operations → quality control → local transfer
& performance tracking → Eco-BIT / CowTalk AI digital follow-up.

> **Hard rule:** the export target is **Korean Holstein sexed dairy embryos only**.
> **Hanwoo genetic export is prohibited and must never be proposed** (see
> `confidentiality-rules.md`).

## Strategic positioning
Position as a **"Korean Dairy Genetics Export Platform" / "K-Dairy Genetics
Improvement Package"** — never as a small export side project. Treat as a future
core business.

1. Sexed Holstein embryo sales
2. Technical service fees
3. Local veterinarian training fees
4. Recipient-cow management consulting
5. Digital follow-up service (Eco-BIT / CowTalk AI)
6. Eco-BIT / CowTalk AI international platform expansion
7. Government cooperation projects (ODA / KOICA / RDA)
8. Long-term genetic-improvement contracts
