# CowTalk AI — Business Context (2 axes)

Shared context for CowTalk AI, smaXtec Sensor Operations, Eco-BIT Platform, and
CEO Strategy agents. Read with `founder-context.md`, `master-context.md`,
`confidentiality-rules.md`.

## CowTalk AI is two business axes
The core of the CowTalk AI business is **not only AI feature development**. It has
two axes:

1. **CowTalk AI integrated digital livestock platform** (software).
2. **smaXtec rumen-sensor supply / installation / operations business** (field).

So CowTalk AI is simultaneously a **software platform business** and a **field
install / operate / manage business** built on smaXtec rumen (bolus) sensors.

## Axis 1 — CowTalk AI integrated platform
Farm dashboard, veterinarian dashboard, inseminator dashboard, government/disease-
control dashboard; reproduction management, disease prediction, calving prediction,
per-animal alerts, per-farm reports, regional statistics; **Eco-BIT platform
integration**.

## Axis 2 — smaXtec rumen-sensor supply & management
Sensor supply; installation scheduling; per-farm installation status; sensor
activation/registration; sensor-data integration; fault/replacement/AS management;
farmer training; monthly subscription / management-fee billing; regional rollout
plan; inventory management; assignment of installers / veterinarians / managers.
→ Operated by the **smaXtec Sensor Operations Agent** (the operations arm of
CowTalk AI). smaXtec Cloud API integration structure is managed here with Eco-BIT.

## Revenue model (8)
1. Rumen-sensor supply revenue
2. Sensor installation revenue
3. Monthly management/subscription fee
4. Per-farm AI report service
5. Veterinarian / inseminator linkage service
6. Per-region platform usage fee (local government)
7. Government demonstration / public-tender projects
8. Eco-BIT expansion-platform revenue

CEO Strategy + CowTalk AI use this model to write business plans, investor
materials, and government proposals.
