# Song Young Shin Farm — 90-Day Growth Plan Workflow

Use to turn the 6 growth goals into a dated 90-day plan centered on
**shop.a2jerseymilk.com**. Owner: Growth Team (lead = Sales & Distribution, with
e-Commerce, Brand Marketing, Product Development).

## Goals (anchor every plan to these)
1. Shop open-ready  2. First 1,000 customers  3. Subscription system
4. Brand build  5. Higher AOV  6. Higher repurchase rate

## Structure
**Phase 1 — Days 0–30: Open-ready**
- e-Commerce: shop IA, main/category/PDP, cart/checkout/membership, review & subscription setup, SEO basics.
- Brand: brand story, hero message, founder/farm story, key PDP copy.
- Product: confirm launch line-up, price/margin, pack/format.
- Sales: pre-launch waitlist, launch offer, first B2B target list.

**Phase 2 — Days 31–60: First customers**
- Acquisition funnel (SNS + KakaoTalk + SEO) → shop.
- Launch subscription with intro offer.
- First cafe/bakery B2B outreach.
- Reviews & UGC engine on.

**Phase 3 — Days 61–90: Retain & raise**
- CRM/email/KakaoTalk retention flows; repurchase campaigns.
- AOV levers (bundles, sets, gifting).
- Department-store / premium food-hall conversations.
- Measure toward 1,000 customers; iterate.

## Required output
- Weekly milestones (12 weeks) with owner agent per task
- KPI targets per phase (visitors, conversion, customers, subscribers, AOV, repurchase)
- Asset checklist per phase
- Risks & dependencies (esp. confirm shop platform — currently 403 externally)
- Next-week actions

## Rule
Every task names an owner agent and a measurable outcome. Mark all numbers as targets.
