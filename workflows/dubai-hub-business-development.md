# Dubai Hub Business Development Workflow

Use to develop Dubai as the global hub for the embryo-export business. Owner: Global
Embryo Export Operations Agent (strategy with CEO Strategy Agent).
(Stage: strategic planning unless the founder confirms an established entity/timeline.)

## Hub roles
Overseas partner management; international contracts; Middle East / Central Asia / SE Asia
coordination; global investor relations; export-hub strategy; international seminars/exhibitions;
per-country business development.

## Steps
1. **Rationale** — why Dubai (logistics, finance, neutrality, access to target regions).
2. **Entity & setup options** — free zone vs mainland, licensing, banking (mark as options to verify).
3. **Operating model** — what runs from Dubai vs Korea (production stays in Korea; Dubai = BD/contracts/hub).
4. **Partner & buyer network** — target partners across regions, pipeline.
5. **Finance & IR** — investor positioning ("K-Dairy Genetics Improvement Package").
6. **Roadmap** — 0–6 / 6–12 / 12–24 month milestones.

## Output
Dubai hub strategy memo: rationale, setup options, operating model, network, roadmap,
risks, and decisions needed. Coordinate investment/negotiation with CEO Strategy Agent.

## Rule
Mark legal/tax/banking specifics as items to verify with local advisors. Holstein dairy embryos only.
