## 2. Professional Summary

A Korean **large-animal (cattle) veterinarian** and **embryo-transfer (ET) specialist with ~18 years of
experience**, performing **about 3,000 embryo transfers and producing/selling about 4,000 embryos per year**
at an average conception rate of ~60%. After obtaining his veterinary license (No. 10193) in 1999 and working
in the feed industry and clinical practice, he **founded Genetics Co., Ltd. in 2009** to lead embryo
production, transfer, and genetic improvement, while serving as **Director of Korea Animal Hospital**.

He holds **full-cycle ET capability** — donor selection, superovulation, in-vivo recovery, OPU, IVF,
freezing/thawing, **sexed embryos**, estrus synchronization, recipient management, and pregnancy diagnosis —
and has a confirmed international record: **since 2023, exporting Korean Holstein sexed dairy embryos to
Uzbekistan with the Rural Development Administration (RDA) — 70 transfers at 55% conception.**

He also operates **Song Young Shin Farm**, certified as **Korea's 1st and 2nd dairy Animal-Welfare Farm** and
a **certified low-carbon dairy farm**, practicing an "odor-free, beautiful farm." He runs a precision-livestock
digital platform (**Eco-BIT/CowTalk AI**) and livestock environmental technology (**D2O**), and serves as an
**Adjunct Professor of Veterinary Medicine (Konkuk University and others)** and in public roles
(**Epidemiology Expert at the former Animal & Plant Quarantine Agency; Gyeonggi-do Animal Welfare Advisor**).
This profile is directly suited to transferring a **"genetics + technology + training + welfare/low-carbon +
digital" genetic-improvement package** to emerging dairy nations such as Nepal.
