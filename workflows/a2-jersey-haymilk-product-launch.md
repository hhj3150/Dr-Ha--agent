# A2 Jersey Hay Milk — Product Launch Workflow

Use to launch an A2 Jersey Hay Milk product (or line extension) on
**shop.a2jerseymilk.com**. Owner: Product Development + Brand Marketing + e-Commerce + Sales.

## Steps
1. **Product definition** (Product Development) — concept, target, formulation direction, pack/format.
2. **Economics** (Product Development) — cost structure, price, margin scenarios.
3. **Manufacturing readiness** (Product Development) — process, parameters, equipment, shelf-life, compliance.
4. **Brand & message** (Brand Marketing) — name, hero message, story, claim discipline.
5. **PDP & shop setup** (e-Commerce) — product-detail page, options, subscription eligibility, reviews.
6. **Launch offer & funnel** (Sales) — intro offer, bundles, subscription hook, B2B angle.
7. **Content plan** (Brand Marketing) — SNS/blog/YouTube launch content.
8. **Go-live checklist** — see ecommerce-launch-checklist for shop-wide readiness.
9. **Post-launch** — track conversion, AOV, repurchase; iterate.

## Required output
- One-page launch brief (concept → price → message → PDP → offer → content)
- Launch-week timeline with owner agents
- KPI targets (units, conversion, AOV, subscription take-rate)
- Compliance check (A2 "may be easier to digest"; lab values only; no medical claims)
- Next actions & data needed

## Rule
Use only provided nutrition lab values. Mark costs/margins as estimates until real costing is supplied.
