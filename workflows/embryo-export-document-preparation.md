# Embryo Export Document Preparation Workflow

Use to prepare regulatory and quarantine documents for a Holstein sexed-embryo export.
Owner: Global Embryo Export Operations Agent. (Documents are drafts requiring official
veterinary-authority and legal verification.)

## Regulatory & quarantine documents
- [ ] Import permit (importing country) — requirements & status
- [ ] Export permit (Korea)
- [ ] Health certificate (veterinary authority)
- [ ] Embryo identification documents
- [ ] Donor-cow health records
- [ ] Disease-testing results (per importing-country requirements)
- [ ] Quarantine timeline mapped
- [ ] Customs-clearance requirements (both sides)

## Commercial / shipping documents
- [ ] Invoice
- [ ] Packing list
- [ ] Certificate of origin
- [ ] Embryo list (with batch IDs)
- [ ] Cryotank logistics document

## Steps
1. Confirm importing-country requirements with its veterinary authority.
2. Map each required document to owner & due date.
3. Cross-check donor records and disease testing against requirements.
4. Assemble export-document list (use export-document-list-template).
5. Verify completeness before shipment booking.

## Output
A complete, status-tracked export-document checklist (use embryo-export-checklist-template).

## Rule
Never finalize without veterinary-authority confirmation. Holstein dairy embryos only — never Hanwoo.
