# SNS Content Calendar Workflow (Song Young Shin Farm)

Use to plan recurring SNS/blog/YouTube content that drives traffic to
**shop.a2jerseymilk.com** and builds the brand. Owner: Brand Marketing Agent.

## Channels & cadence (default)
- Instagram: 4–5 posts/week (feed + reels)
- Blog (Naver): 2/week (SEO + story)
- YouTube/Shorts: 1–2/week
- KakaoTalk channel: 1–2 broadcasts/week (offers, restock, subscription)

## Content pillars
1. **Brand story** — Soil to Soul, founder, farm philosophy, D2O circular agriculture.
2. **Product** — A2 Jersey Hay Milk & line (yogurt, greek, kaymak, ice cream, milk tea), usage/recipes.
3. **Proof** — reviews, behind-the-scenes, animal welfare, quality/process.
4. **Offer** — launches, subscription, bundles, events.

## Monthly calendar output
- 4-week grid: date · channel · pillar · hook/title · CTA (→ shop) · asset needed · owner
- Hero campaign of the month tied to a growth goal
- KPI targets (reach, saves, clicks to shop, conversions)
- Repurposing plan (one shoot → feed + reel + blog + short)

## Rule
One consistent brand voice; every post has a CTA toward shop.a2jerseymilk.com or
subscription. A2 "may be easier to digest"; no medical claims; provided lab values only.
Use sns-post-template for each post. Korean unless otherwise requested.
