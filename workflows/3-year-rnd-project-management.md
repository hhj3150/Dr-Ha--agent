# 3-Year R&D Project Management Workflow

Use to manage our 3-year MOTIE (산자부) participating-company R&D project end to end.
Owner: Government R&D Secretary Agent.

## Structure
**Year 1 / Year 2 / Year 3** — for each year track:
- Annual objective & quantitative goals
- Monthly task plan vs actual
- Deliverables (prototype / paper / patent / certification / sales)
- Performance indicators (target vs achieved)
- Budget execution (인건비 / 재료비 / 외주비 / 장비비)
- 주관기관 requests & responses
- Interim/annual report due dates

## Cadence
- Monthly: task summary + progress report (see monthly-rnd-progress-report)
- Quarterly: indicator review + evaluation readiness
- Annually: annual report + settlement (정산) prep
- Final year: final report + final evaluation slides

## Required output
- 36-month timeline with annual milestones
- Indicator tracker linkage (performance-indicator-tracker)
- Settlement checklist linkage (rnd-expense-checklist)
- Risk & deadline list
- Next-month actions with owners

## Rule
Keep traceable records and accurate dates. Mark unconfirmed figures as draft.
Coordinate technical content with the relevant technical agent.
