# Dealer / Distributor Recruitment Plan Workflow

Use to recruit and manage dealers (대리점) and regional distributors (총판) for
peat-moss products. Owner: D2O Peat Moss Commercial Agent.

## Steps
1. Define territory map (regions, priority).
2. Define dealer profile (existing ag/livestock supply, customer base, logistics).
3. Define offer: margin, MOQ, exclusivity terms, support (samples, education, marketing).
4. Build recruitment pitch + dealer-recruitment proposal.
5. Onboarding: pricing, ordering, training, sales materials.
6. Performance management: monthly targets, reorder cadence, support cadence.

## Required output
- Territory & priority list
- Dealer profile & qualification criteria
- Margin/terms structure (draft)
- Recruitment proposal (use dealer-recruitment-template)
- Onboarding & support plan
- Monthly target & KPI per dealer

## Rule
Win-win economics; protect brand positioning and price discipline. Mark terms as draft.
