# Researcher / Staff Management Workflow

Use to manage Genetics lab researchers and staff. Owner: Genetics Lab Operations Agent.

## Scope
Per-researcher duties; weekly assignment; monthly roster; per-experiment owner; training;
new-researcher onboarding; SOP training; performance-criteria draft; hiring plan; job
descriptions; task-omission-prevention checklist.

## Steps
1. **Roles & duties** — map each researcher to responsibilities (job description).
2. **Weekly assignment** — assign experiments/tasks (use researcher-work-assignment-template).
3. **Monthly roster** — work schedule, coverage, leave.
4. **Onboarding** — new-researcher plan (use staff-onboarding-template) + SOP training (use lab-sop-template).
5. **Training & safety** — training materials, lab-safety education.
6. **Performance** — draft evaluation criteria; hiring plan if understaffed.
7. **Omission prevention** — checklist so no critical task is dropped.

## Output
Job descriptions, weekly assignment table, monthly roster, onboarding pack, training/safety
materials, evaluation-criteria draft, hiring plan, omission-prevention checklist.

## Rule
Keep assignments explicit and traceable; avoid task gaps in production/QC. Drafts for HR/performance
items require management review.
