# Genetics Lab Quality Control Workflow

Use to control quality of produced embryos, especially export-grade sexed embryos.
Owner: Genetics Lab Operations Agent.

## QC scope
- Embryo grading & stage records
- Sexing verification process
- Freezing/vitrification quality
- Cryostorage integrity (LN2 tanks, inventory accuracy)
- Media/reagent lot control & expiry
- Equipment calibration

## Steps
1. Define quality criteria per grade and per export requirement.
2. Record grade & stage for each embryo/batch (use embryo-batch-inventory-template).
3. Verify sexing and freezing quality; document.
4. Check cryostorage integrity & inventory reconciliation.
5. Lot/expiry control for media & reagents.
6. Handle claims/quality issues with root-cause & corrective action.

## Output
A QC checklist result (use lab-qc-checklist-template), batch grade records, and any
corrective-action notes for export readiness.

## Rule
Export batches must meet importing-country and contract quality criteria. Mark uncertain
biological outcomes as expected; recommend veterinary oversight.
