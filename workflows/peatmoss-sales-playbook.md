# Peat Moss Sales Playbook Workflow

Use to drive peat-moss revenue across farm species and channels. Owner: D2O Peat
Moss Commercial Agent (tech effect from D2O Environment Carbon Agent).

## Segments
Dairy / Hanwoo / swine / poultry farms; compost plants; fertilizer factories; 농협 / 축협;
local governments; dealers (대리점) / distributors (총판).

## Per-species usage scenarios
For each species define: bedding depth/refresh, moisture & odor control, manure/compost
benefit, and the resulting ROI vs sawdust (톱밥 대체).

## Pipeline
1. Target list (by species & region)
2. Qualify (herd size, current bedding, pain points)
3. Proposal + quote + usage scenario (use farm-proposal-workflow)
4. Trial / sample
5. Close (price, MOQ, delivery)
6. Repeat-purchase & account growth

## Channel plays
- Direct farm sales
- Dealer/distributor recruitment (see dealer-recruitment-plan)
- 농협/축협 supply proposals
- Local-government support-program linkage
- Expo/exhibition sales

## Required output
- Segmented target list
- Per-species ROI scenarios
- Pricing tiers (draft) & quote
- Pipeline tracker + monthly sales targets/KPIs
- Next sales actions

## Rule
Sell outcomes (odor↓, welfare↑, compost value↑, labor↓). Describe Dr. Ha Liquid by
function only. Mark prices as draft until confirmed.
