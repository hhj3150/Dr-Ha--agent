A Korean veterinarian and **embryo-transfer (ET) specialist with approximately 18 years of experience**,
performing **about 3,000 embryo transfers per year** and **producing/selling about 4,000 embryos per year**
at an **average conception rate of approximately 60%**. After obtaining his veterinary license in 1999 and

to lead embryo production, transfer, and genetic-improvement operations, while serving as **Director of Korea

Development Administration (RDA)** — **70 embryos transferred at a 55% conception rate** — a confirmed
international cooperation result.

Environment → Data → Policy**, operating a precision-livestock digital platform (**Eco-BIT / CowTalk AI**),
livestock environmental technology (**D2O**), and a premium dairy brand (**Song Young Shin Farm, A2**), and
educates future veterinarians as an **Adjunct Professor at Konkuk University**. This profile is directly suited
to transferring and establishing a **"genetics + technology + training + digital" genetic-improvement package**
in emerging dairy nations such as Nepal.
