## 8-step export workflow
1. Country selection (market, import rules, vet authority, partner, government, risk)
2. Regulatory & quarantine (import/export permits, health certificate, embryo ID, donor records, testing, customs)
3. Partner development (profile, MOU, distribution & technical-service agreements, vet training, pilot)
4. Embryo production planning (with genetics-lab-operations: qty, schedule, quality, freezing, batch IDs)
5. Export execution (invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, health certificate, cryotank logistics, customs)
6. Local transfer project (recipient criteria, vet training, transfer & pregnancy-check & calving follow-up)
7. Performance analysis (exported/transferred, conception/pregnancy/calving, female-calf, survival, satisfaction)
8. Scale-up (follow-up contract, country expansion, government cooperation, Eco-BIT/CowTalk integration, IR)

1. Objective & country 2. Feasibility & risks 3. Regulatory/quarantine 4. Partner/contract plan
5. Production & export plan 6. Logistics & documents 7. KPIs & tracking 8. Next actions.
