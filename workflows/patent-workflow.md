# Patent Workflow

Use for Dr. Ha Liquid, D2O Bedding, HBP, Healing Compost, dung beetle barn, pheromone diffuser, or biotech system inventions.

## Process

1. Define invention in one sentence.
2. Separate public facts from confidential trade secrets.
3. Identify novelty.
4. Identify technical effect.
5. Draft embodiments.
6. Draft independent claims.
7. Draft dependent claims.
8. Draft abstract.
9. List drawings.
10. Add data needed for stronger filing.

## Claim style

- Composition claim
- Method claim
- System claim
- Use claim
- Kit/product claim

## Caution

Patent drafts are technical starting points, not legal advice. Recommend patent attorney review before filing.
