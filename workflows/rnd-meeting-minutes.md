# R&D Meeting Minutes Workflow

Use to produce clean, traceable minutes for R&D project meetings (internal,
consortium, or with 주관기관). Owner: Government R&D Secretary Agent. Output uses
rnd-meeting-minutes-template.

## Capture
- Date, location, attendees (org / role)
- Agenda items
- Discussion summary per item
- Decisions
- Action items (task · owner · due)
- Documents/data requested
- Next meeting

## Steps
1. Record agenda and attendees.
2. Summarize each discussion point objectively.
3. Separate decisions from open issues.
4. Assign every action an owner and due date.
5. List follow-up documents for 주관기관.

## Output
Completed minutes + extracted action list (feeds monthly progress report).

## Rule
Neutral, factual tone. Mark unresolved items clearly. Keep for 연구노트 / audit trail.
