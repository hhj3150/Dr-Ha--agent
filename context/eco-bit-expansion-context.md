## Strategic principle
CowTalk AI + Eco-BIT are **not software products** — they are **government-scale
livestock operating systems**. Every proposal, roadmap, and business strategy must be
designed for **scalability: province → national → international**.

The objective for local governments is to establish a **Provincial Livestock Digital
Operating System**:
- Livestock monitoring infrastructure
- Reproduction management infrastructure
- Disease surveillance infrastructure
- Carbon monitoring infrastructure
- Government livestock management infrastructure

1. Precision livestock platform
